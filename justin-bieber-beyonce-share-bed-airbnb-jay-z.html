OK, you can relax. Justin and Beyonce may have slept in the same bed, but it was definitely at different times. Thanks to Airbnb, Justin recently rented a hillside mansion in San Francisco’s Bay Area — the very same one Beyonce rented during the Super Bowl in February.

The 22-year-old posted a picture of himself on the edge of the house’s pool on Wednesday, March 17, with the caption, “bless up.” The home reportedly costs $10,000 to $30,000 a night — but Justin got is for free as Airbnb picked up the tab!

The home, which features an infinity pool, has five bedrooms and eight bathrooms, so it’s more than big enough for any pop star. Even Beyonce, who posted pictures, highlighting the home’s beauty, when she stayed there a few weeks ago. In one photo, she relaxed by the fire pit wrapped up in warm furry blanket. Did we mention the home has a private orchard? Well, it does. Plus, it has a rooftop vegetable and fruit garden, according to Daily Mail.
