Who Is Samatha Logan?

Samantha Logan, also known as Sammy, was born on October 27, 1996, and is an artist, model, and performer from the United States. She is of Trinidadian and Irish ancestry.

She is well-known for her roles in films such as 666 Park Avenue, Nona Clark, All American, and 13 Reasons Why. She was born in the Boston neighborhood of Massachusetts, and her heritage is Trinidadian-Irish, to be specific.
