Meet Mae Stephens, The Hitmaker Behind TikTok Hit If We Ever Broke Up

Mae Stephens is the voice on the viral TikTok son If We Ever Broke Up. The song has been taking over the platform by storm since December 2022. Mae Stephens Boyfriend

Mae Stephens had a boyfriend and even wrote a song for him after the breakup. According to her TikTok, her ex told her that she couldn’t do better. She made the song to put on the radio so that she could tell her ex that she did better.
