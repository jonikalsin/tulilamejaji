Chyna Mills Job

Chyna Mills worked as a Youth Support Worker before she appeared on Love Island.

For those who don’t know, a youth support worker actively participates in community-based campaigns geared at bolstering support within young, vulnerable cohorts. They are also known as youth facilitators or youth care workers.

Requirements for being a Youth Support Worker include a High school diploma (which Chyna got from Roundhay High School), and possession of applicable job-related qualifications such as knowledge about the most threatening social determinants of vulnerability.

An average Youth Support Worker’s salary in the UK was around £25 (around $30) thousand per annum. So, this was probably what Chyna made as well.
