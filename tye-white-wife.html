Tye White Wife Rania Nasreen White Is An Actress

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Tye White and Rania Nasreen had a beautiful Jamaican destination wedding on June 18, 2018.

White and Nasreen said 'I Do' in Ocho Rios, Jamaica. The bride was dressed in a trumpet wedding gown which featured a cathedral veil, she completed her look with a bouquet of pink roses.

Her groom wore a grey and white tuxedo completing his look with a beautiful pink rose boutonnière matching his bride's bouquet.

Nasreen shared an image of her father walking her down the aisle on her Instagram saying she feared the day would never come. Her father previously suffered from brain cancer but was declared free of the disease at the age of 70 years old. 

In other images shared on Nasreen's Instagram, the newlyweds looked all loved up. Tye shared images with his groomsmen on his picture-sharing social media account.

It took six years of dating before White and Nasreen could finally tie the knot. They started dating in 2012 and Tye decided to add to his 2017 new year celebration by putting an engagement ring on Rania's finger.

Tye shared the news with his followers by sharing the image of the sparkling engagement ring while he held Rania's hand.

The couple was engaged for a little over one and a half years before their nuptials. However, they have not shared anything about their first meeting with their fans and followers.
