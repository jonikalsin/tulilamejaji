Things are about to change for the Carolina Panthers.

After finishing 7-10 in the 2022 regular season, the team decided to trade for the No. 1 overall pick with the Chicago Bears.

Now that they’ve secured their franchise quarterback in Bryce Young, all the pieces are in place for head coach Frank Reich’s ascendant squad.

All that’s missing is you.

Thankfully, Panthers fans can pick up tickets for all home games at Bank of America Stadium in Charlotte to see Young along with star running back Miles Sanders, wide receiver Adam Thielen, tight end Hayden Hurst and rookie wideout Jonathan Mingo.

That includes tix for huge matchups against the Vikings on Oct. 1, the Cowboys on Nov. 19 and the Packers on Dec. 24.

How to watch/stream Panthers games

There is no shortage of ways to watch Panthers games live from the comfort of your own home this season.

You can find Reich’s squad on ABC, CBS, ESPN, Fox, NBC and the NFL Network on select dates.

Through the regular season, most NFC games will be broadcast on Fox.

For specialty games, Sunday Night Football can be found on NBC along with Peacock, Monday Night Football is reserved for ESPN and Thursday Night Football streams on Amazon Prime Video.

Carolina Panthers news

If you’re not familiar with Young yet, we’re here to help get you acquainted.

Young, 21, played college ball at Alabama and was named the 2021 Heisman Trophy winner.

“The Bryce Youngs don’t come around very often where you get somebody that — doesn’t fit all the specs but — is supremely talented,” NFL Network analyst Daniel Jeremiah said.
