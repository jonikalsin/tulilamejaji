Who was Cole Pensick?

Cole Pensick was the son of Dan and Bev Pensick. Born on October 10, 1990, he earned his bachelor’s degree from Nebraska in December of 2012. He entered the Nebraska Scholar-Athlete Honor Roll in the fall and spring semesters of 2012-13.

Cole was a key member of the Husker offensive line in 2013 and the years before whenever he played. He played in all 13 games and started in 12 out of them. His incredible performance helped him earn a second-team All-Big Ten choice by the league coaches.

Before coming to Nebraska, Pensick was admitted to Lincoln Northeast High School. He was the first player to commit to Nebraska in 2009 class was picked as a defensive tackle. He worked on the scout team in 2009. Cole also excelled at professional wrestling.

Cole Pensick died in a Car Accident: What happened?
